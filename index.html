About Us

KOVI, founded in 2024 in Hong Kong, is a pioneering brand with a mission to promote clothing independence through innovation and accessibility. With a focus on providing high-quality sportswear, KOVI seeks to empower individuals by offering versatile and functional apparel designed for modern, active lifestyles. Our commitment to sustainability, inclusivity, and self-expression is at the core of our brand, ensuring that our clothing is not only comfortable and durable but also adaptable to diverse needs and preferences. Whether you’re hitting the gym, enjoying outdoor activities, or simply embracing a more dynamic approach to everyday fashion, KOVI’s collections aim to inspire confidence and freedom in every movement.

At KOVI, we believe in redefining the relationship between people and their clothing, fostering a sense of independence where our customers can feel empowered to dress for their active lives without compromise.
