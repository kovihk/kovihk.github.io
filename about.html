THE BRAND

Based in Paris, VAILLANT is a french luxury women's ready-to-wear brand founded by Alice Vaillant.

The brand offers a hybrid and empowered wardrobe, and strives to embody a strong, sensual and disruptive vision of femininity.

 
 

THE AESTHETIC

Being part of the Paris Opera Ballet for ten years, classical dance has shaped a large part of Aliceâ€™s aesthetic sense that revolves around performance and movement. Her designs are immersed in this language, but also simultaneously break free from these classic ballet codes.

The collections are focused on rigidity, on one hand, but at the same time deconstruct this rigidity on the other. They explore movement and contemporary form for a fundamental play with opposites: the feminine and the masculine, motion and constraint, openness and rigor, free-flow and direction.

Alice works with the layering of materials, mixing technical pieces and free flowing styles. She displays geometrical and architectural silhouettes that are contrasted with fluid fabrics and materials. She works with contrast in either color, shape, or cut. Aliceâ€™s creations are easily recognizable by her use of lace, which is an integral part of the brandâ€™s DNA and also serves as a strong and essential visual code for VAILLANT.

After moving on from dance, she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Fashion Design in Montreal. In 2018 she obtained her Masters of Arts in womenswear in Paris.
